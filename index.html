Services

Clear, inspection-led support that improves evidence quality and reduces late quality surprises.

Governance & boundaries

LocArc provides independent inspection, verification and pre-assurance support only. We do not hold approval authority and do not replace the roles of the contractor, main contractor, designer, or client.

Summary: Advisory and verification support • No approval/sign-off • No audit/enforcement role • No change to contractual responsibility.

Risk-ranked findings Clear priority list: what will cause delays / NCRs if left unresolved.
Corrective action recommendations Practical steps to close gaps and strengthen readiness.
